Large Language Model Psychometrics: A Comprehensive Survey of Evaluation, Validation, and Enhancement

Haoran Ye, A, B, C
Peking university
Paper Code arXiv

This paper presents a comprehensive survey of evaluation methods for large language models, exploring the intersections between traditional psychometric approaches and modern LLM evaluation techniques. We review current validation methods, propose enhancements to existing frameworks, and discuss the future directions of LLM evaluation methodologies.
